environmental impact

who do we serve

the community

We want to serve the Apapa community located in Lagos state, Nigeria . Apapa is the dirtiest area in the state, containing several ports and terminals with unconventional waste, potholes, and littered roads. It is essential because this is an area with a large mass of water that is already affected by water, air, and land pollution, which affects people's well-being and the overall functioning of the ports; therefore, there is a need for recycling because it is an opportunity for us to improve the environment and also create a better living atmosphere. In doing this, people can access clean water from the port and safely dock their ships
